Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VerifAI

What is VerifAI?
VerifAI is an AI-powered platform designed to detect and classify fake or misleading news using advanced content analysis and source credibility verification. We help users identify misinformation before it spreads.
How accurate is the AI detection?
Our AI models achieve approximately 98% accuracy rate based on millions of analyzed articles. However, we always recommend cross-referencing important information with multiple trusted sources for critical decisions.
What types of content can I verify?
VerifAI currently supports text articles and images. You can paste news articles, social media posts, or upload images to check for manipulations, deepfakes, and verify their authenticity.
Is my data stored or shared?
No, we prioritize your privacy. Content you submit for verification is analyzed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers. We do not share your data with third parties.
How do I interpret credibility scores?
Scores range from 0-100%. Higher scores (above 60%) indicate likely credible content. Scores below 60% suggest potential misinformation. The score is based on multiple factors including source reputation, content consistency, and historical accuracy patterns.
Can I verify content in different languages?
Currently, VerifAI works best with English content. We're actively working on expanding our language support to include Spanish, French, German, and other major languages in future updates.
